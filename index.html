Who are we ?

Stitch Apparels is a company of insight, having in-depth knowledge and success in garment manufacturing. Our company was established with a belief to serve the garment industry and live up to the expectations of our clients. We manufacture and supply custom knitwear garments like sportswear, athleisure , fashion apparel, etc and we also produce woven-wear garments for different field like construction, mines, oil refineries, military etc.
Our office is based in Rowland Heights, California, USA while our entire production is made in our factory facility in Lahore, Pakistan. With our expertise in manufacturing, we produce garments for various brands all over the world and support them in developing their collections.

Mission Statement

To treat everyone with honesty, respect, fairness, trust and loyalty. Working as a team to develop and produce a great product with feelings and emotions attached to it. The Success of mission allows us to support our clients and team for great product and longevity.

Services

Stitch Apparels has established offices in California USA, Berlin Germany, and London UK. Which plays a vital role in strong connection, communication and understanding between both parties without any cultural or language barriers.
Stitch Apparels have a well established vertically integrated manufacturing facility in Lahore, Pakistan. Our Facility is able to produce large bulk orders and we also serve startups and small business to produce high quality products and assist them in development and research.

Research & Development

Stitch Apparels has a team of smart and creative thinkers. Who are always looking for new fabrics, trims, accessories, embellishments and other techniques which help our clients to add up new products and stand out in the market.

Design & Prototype

We offer Design and Prototype facility to our customers who just come up with an idea and want to execute it to enter into the market. With our team of Fashion Designers, Garment technologists, graphic designers. We are able to assist our clients build up the new products from scratch along with the Blueprints of the product like (Tech Pack, Mock Up, Specs sheet, etc.).

Manufacturing

Our Manufacturing team consist of skilled and critical thinkers on both management level and worker level which helps us assuring the quality standards. Our Management head and team takes care of sampling, pre-production sampling, scouring quality trims, fabric, materials for embellishment, bulk production, quality control and logistics.
